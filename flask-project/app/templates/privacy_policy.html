Privacy Policy

How Med Reminder uses and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

For this project, Med Reminder stores only the information needed to manage your account and medication reminders, such as your email address, basic profile information, and medication schedule.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to send reminders, display your medication list, and provide notification settings. We do not sell or share your information with third parties as part of this demo application.

3. Data Security

Access to your account is protected by a password. Make sure you keep your login details safe and sign out when using shared devices.

4. Your Choices

You can change your email, adjust notification settings, and request account deletion from the Settings section of the app.
