Guidelines for using the Med Reminder application.

1. Purpose of the App

Med Reminder is a student project designed to help users remember their medications. It is provided for educational purposes and does not replace professional medical advice.

2. User Responsibility

You are responsible for entering accurate medication information and verifying reminders. Always follow the advice of your doctor or pharmacist.

3. No Medical Guarantees

While the app attempts to send reminders reliably, missed notifications can happen due to device settings, network issues, or other factors. Always double-check your medication schedule.

4. Changes to These Terms

Because this is a project, the design and features may change over time as part of development and evaluation.
